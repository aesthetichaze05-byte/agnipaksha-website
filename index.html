Who We Are

AGNIPAKSHA is a student-led aerospace engineering team focused on the design, development, and testing of competition-grade sounding rockets.

Our work spans propulsion systems, avionics and telemetry, vehicle structures, and recovery systems, following industry-grade aerospace engineering practices.

Contact: agnipaksha.aero@university.edu · Instagram

Capabilities

Propulsion Systems

Solid motors, thrust analysis, static testing

  • Motor selection & certification
  • Thrust curve simulation
  • Static fire test planning & safety
  • Nozzle & grain geometry analysis

Avionics & Telemetry

Custom flight computers, sensors, telemetry

  • Custom flight computer design
  • Sensor integration (IMU, barometer)
  • Real-time telemetry (LoRa)
  • Ground station software

Structures & Manufacturing

Airframe design, CAD, simulations

  • Airframe CAD modeling
  • Material selection
  • Structural simulations
  • Manufacturing & assembly

Recovery Systems

Dual-deployment, reusability

  • Dual-deployment recovery
  • Parachute sizing
  • Deployment electronics
  • Safe reusability

Current Mission

Design, build, and launch a competition-grade sounding rocket targeting 3 km altitude.
